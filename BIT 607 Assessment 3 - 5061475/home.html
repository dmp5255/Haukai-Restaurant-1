Welcome to The Haukai Restaurant

The Haukai Restaurant is a family friendly, locally owned, modern and casual bistro that specializes in local fare. We pride ourselves on serving beautifully presented dishes, prompt and professional customer service, as well as a clean and comfortable premise for you and your guests. Our decor evokes an energetic and welcoming atmosphere.

The Haukai Restaurant promises to provide exceptional service and delicious, fresh, and creative food. Be sure to stop in whether it’s for a quick bite, breakfast, brunch, lunch, take-out, a special occasion, or just to say hello.

The Haukai Restaurant promises to cater to all including, children, vegetarians, those with a gluten intolerance, and those with food allergies. Give us a try, you won’t regret it! From all of us at The Haukai Restaurant, we can’t wait to meet and serve you!
